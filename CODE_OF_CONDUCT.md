# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We expect all contributors to keep discussions technical, respectful, and
constructive.

## Expected behavior

- Focus on issues, data, and code.
- Assume good intent and ask for clarification before escalating.
- Keep review feedback specific and actionable.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Repeated bad-faith arguments or disruptive behavior.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block participation for
violations.

## Contact

Open an issue tagged `governance` for concerns related to this policy.
